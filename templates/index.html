DeepScan is a deepfake image detection system developed to address the growing risks of manipulated visual content in cybersecurity and digital forensics. The system combines MTCNN-based face detection with a fine-tuned VGG16 deep learning model to accurately classify images as real or fake. To enhance transparency, Grad-CAM is integrated to provide visual explanations highlighting the regions that influence the model’s decision. The platform is designed as a lightweight, web-based application that delivers fast and reliable results while maintaining user privacy, as no uploaded images are stored. DeepScan aims to support analysts, researchers, and everyday users in verifying the authenticity of digital images and reducing the impact of deepfake-based threats.
